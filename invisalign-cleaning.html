Dr. Holmes' Daily Cleaning Routine

The step-by-step guide to keeping your aligners fresh and clean

1

Remove & Rinse

Remove aligners and rinse immediately with lukewarm water to prevent saliva from drying.

2

Gentle Brush

Use a soft-bristled toothbrush with water only (no toothpaste) to gently remove debris.

3

Soak Daily

Soak in Polident solution for 15 minutes while you eat breakfast or dinner.

4

Final Rinse

Rinse thoroughly with water before putting aligners back in your mouth.
