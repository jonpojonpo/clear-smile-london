Why ClinCheck Planning Makes or Breaks Your Treatment

The digital planning stage determines 80% of your treatment success

What Experienced Dentists Do

Spend 2-3 hours refining the ClinCheck plan

Carefully plan attachment placement for optimal movement

Consider root movement and biological limitations

Stage movements for predictable, gentle progress

Plan for realistic outcomes, not marketing promises

What High-Volume Providers Do

Accept Invisalign's initial ClinCheck with minimal review

Rush through planning to start treatment quickly

Place generic attachment patterns without customization

Focus on volume over treatment quality

Promise unrealistic timelines based on initial plan

Common Challenges During Treatment

What they don't warn you about (and how to handle it)

Speech Changes

Temporary lisp or speech adjustment for first 1-2 weeks. Practice reading aloud to adapt faster.

Duration: 1-3 weeks typically

Tracking Issues

Teeth not moving as planned. May require additional aligners, longer wear time, or treatment restart.

Frequency: 15-20% of patients

Lost Aligners

Wrapped in tissue, thrown away accidentally. Always use your case! Replacement costs Â£150-300.

Prevention: Always use protective case

Compliance Fatigue

Months 6-12 motivation drops. 22+ hours daily becomes challenging. Support systems help.

Peak time: Months 8-14

Attachment Breakage

Small bumps can pop off during removal. Usually requires clinic visit to replace and continue treatment.

Cost: Usually included in treatment

Social Situations

Eating out, work meetings, special events. Planning removal/cleaning becomes part of daily routine.

Tip: Carry cleaning kit everywhere

What Makes Treatment Successful

The factors that determine whether you love your results

Critical Success Factors

Compliance (22+ hours daily)

The #1 factor. Even 20 hours isn't enough for optimal results. Consistency is everything.

Proper Treatment Planning

Comprehensive ClinCheck planning determines success. Experienced dentists spend more time perfecting the digital plan - this effort directly impacts your results.

Regular Monitoring

Catching tracking issues early prevents major problems later. In-person check-ups matter.

Patient Support

Having someone to call when problems arise. Motivation during difficult periods.
